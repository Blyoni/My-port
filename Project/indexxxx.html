Who We Are

A Proven Leader in Utility Engineering,

HBK Engineering, LLC is a fully licensed, professional engineering design firm headquartered in downtown Chicago, with a dedicated team of over 600 staff.

What We Do

HBK provides civil, environmental, structural, electrical, subsurface utility, construction support services and permitting facilitation.

Our Reputation

Since our founding in 1998, we have earned a reputation for delivering outstanding, accurate services in extremely time-sensitive, multilayered environments. That "industry-recognized" performance has propelled our East Coast expansion and subsequent licensure to practice in 47 states.

Our Value

With offices Nationwide, including concentrations in the Midwest and on the Coasts, HBK brings together a dedicated team whose main focus is the design and protection of utilities and utility networks. We provide solutions and construction support on utility projects in rural and complex urban centers.

Quanta Services is the leading, end-to-end solutions provider in the electric power sector. We build the infrastructure that powers your world.

Quanta engineers, constructs and maintains everything from generating stations, substations, transmission lines of any size and distribution lines. With companies around the globe and the most highly skilled and trained workforce in the industry, we deliver world-class execution on a local level.

Quanta Services can self-perform any size job in the underground utility sector.

With companies across North America, we offer a full slate of services, including full engineering and design skills through project management ranging from large cross-country pipelines to distribution construction, pipeline integrity, liquid pipelines and facilities, pump stations, tank farms, terminals, joint trench and much more. We also provide comprehensive solutions across the refining, petrochemical, midstream and upstream sectors.

Quanta Services offers a full range of communications capabilities across North America and Australia.

We offer services ranging from engineering and design, program management, turnkey fiber installation, wireless and make ready capabilities. We also offer a full slate of communications training at the Quanta Advanced Training Center at the Lazy Q Ranch in La Grange, Texas.
